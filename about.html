About Us

Kape-Sipingan is dedicated to providing you with the best online shopping experience.

Welcome to Kape-Sipingan! We began in 2024 with the aim of bringing the rich tradition of coffee to life. Our name, 'Siping,' meaning 'to be together,' symbolizes our mission to connect people through coffee. Also, the F5 in our logo, symbolizing the dash, represents "Refresh" on the keyboard. As .NET Programming students, we want to add our knowledge into to the business we've created, and drinking our coffee serves as a refreshing experience. Every cup holds a story, and we pour our hearts into crafting each one. Thank you for being part of our journey!

We pride ourselves on our commitment to customer service and satisfaction. We are here to help you find what you need and make sure you get the best possible service.
